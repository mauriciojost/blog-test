Writing a post or a presentation? Both!

Post or slides?

Presentations are great. Blogs too.

Which one do I dedicate my time to?

Why choosing? Write both at once!

In this special post/presentation, I will give you an example with sources on how to build your own blog posts, and generate both:

  • a post, and
  • a presentation

from a single markdown file.

You can have text that will be ignored in the slides, but written in the post, so you can go more into details where it really matters.
